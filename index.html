Flowers are the eyes of nature - purple velvet violets, blue bells, blue forget-me-nots and small daisy stars with white eyelashes raise their heads to the sun, rejoicing in warmth and light.Every time you see proud lilies or regal roses, you cannot help but wonder how perfect plants can be, how different and beautiful they are in their own way: the brightness of color, the elegance of buds and the miracle of blossoming petals.

In early spring, nature gives us a piece of bright blue sky, when tiny hyacinths bloom, and shards of the sun, which are reminiscent of the yellow caps of dandelions. White lilies of the valley, hidden by huge juicy green leaves, appear already in early May, and these are the most long-awaited spring flowers, bouquets of which we are happy to give to our friends and loved ones when we are happy and want to share a good mood.
